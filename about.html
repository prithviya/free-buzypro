The BuzyPro Story

Our journey from a small consulting firm to Indias trusted business compliance partner

2008

Where It All Began

BuzzPro started as a small tax consulting firm founded by a team of experienced chartered accountants and legal experts with a vision to simplify compliance for Indian businesses.

2012

Expanding Horizons

With a growing client base, we expanded our services to include company registration, trademark filing, and other legal compliance services to become a one-stop solution.

2016

Digital Transformation

We embraced technology to streamline our processes, launching our digital platform to make business compliance accessible to entrepreneurs across India, even in remote areas.

2018

GST Revolution

When the GST reform was introduced, we helped over 500 businesses transition smoothly to the new tax regime with our expertise and support.

Looking Forward

Our journey is just beginning. Were committed to continuously innovating and expanding our services to meet the evolving needs of Indian businesses.
